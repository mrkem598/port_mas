About

ServiceNowStar headquarter is located in Washington, DC. We are a group of volunteers virtually connected together from all over the world to solve key global issues. ServiceNowStar is working in the advancement of the younger generation with a prime source of quality educational information free of charge, training, empowerment, capacity building to positively impact the generation around the world. The company dedicated to providing mental training, offering workshops to empower the young age locally. The company designed to prepare students on how to succeed in high school to colleges and furthermore in their first job by boosting their confidence, showing professional clues, and tips to win in an interview.

This company is also working on to solve problems associated with the lack of experience which is entirely required by the recruiter. The company offers these services virtually and also by having the live workshop in Washington D.C. The seminars will be a base for interacting with others, creating connections, and learning from experienced professionals how they tackle such challenging issues in their career. In each seminar, there is an invited speaker including government officials. ServiceNowStar is delivering it's stretched activity with individually curated training resources to get the required skills and knowledge to succeed in a technical interview. With the continued success of ServiceNowStar, more and more young adults will be attracted to using their smart devices to learn from the internet and build their capability. With the ServiceNowStar Global Community Partnership, the company works with fresh developers to boost their experience by creating an opportunity to develop an application for developing countries virtually. Involving the recent graduate and job seekers will help them to network, learn agile development approach from home, cooperate in critical matters, and broadly have authentic development experience to showcase in their resume.
